PREDATOR
One of our core strengths is our fully integrated in-house IT division, our logistics management solutions, which, thanks to optimal flexibility, allows us to quickly and efficiently customize our IT systems to fully respond to the specifics requirements of our client's own IT systems. We aim to maximize integration of the core IT processes within the supply chain between logistics provider and our client and seek out the smoothest and most efficient integration at the system interface. This is done through our full-customizable, sector-first ‘Predator’ software platform which can integrate add-on functions such as external stock checking, customs, taxation, shipping or movements. Our system is able to track parts in correlation with the consumption of the plant, based on a made-to-order process.

Our fully bespoke supply chain solutions encompass all aspects of logistics provision. We will analyze your operations, identify and address logistic inefficiencies and areas for improvement and seek to unlock additional value, optimize sequencing in the process bring our unrivalled expertise to every corner of your chain. We can manage all required aspects of the logistics process such as customs, taxation, insurance, stock level requirements, recycling and 'green' solutions, zero waste management and packaging.
SUB ASSEMBLY
In another sector-first, we can manage specific sub-assembly or assembly operations within the chain. Our flexibility and expertise base allows us to maximize our warehousing space and sequencing operations to remove certain secondary tasks from the client’s assembly plant. Sub assembly that we current carry out includes wheel and tire fitment and balancing, subframe initial assembly with additional components and partial door assembly. These components are then delivered into the supply chain ready for final integration, having removed non-essential task burdens from the assembly plant, freeing up capacity and with cost reductions.
